Recipe: Lasagna

Home

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and melted cheese—typically mozzarella and Parmesan. Baked until golden and bubbling, lasagna is hearty, satisfying, and perfect for family dinners or special occasions. With countless variations—from traditional Bolognese to vegetarian and gluten-free options—lasagna is a versatile dish that brings bold flavors and delicious textures to the table.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 can (28 oz / 796 mL) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • 1 container (15 oz / 425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tsp dried)
  • 9-12 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Extra Parmesan for topping

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Sauté chopped onion for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  3. Add minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon. Drain excess fat if needed.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, pepper, and optional sugar. Simmer uncovered for 20-30 minutes.
  6. If using regular lasagna noodles, cook them in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and lay flat to cool.
  7. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, grated Parmesan, salt, pepper, and parsley until smooth.
  8. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  9. Spread 1/2 to 1 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  10. Add a layer of noodles (3-4 side by side).
  11. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  12. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded mozzarella over the ricotta.
  13. Repeat layers (sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella) two more times.
  14. Top with remaining sauce and extra mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  15. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  16. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  17. Let lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
